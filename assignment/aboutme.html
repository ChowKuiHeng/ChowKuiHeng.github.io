About Me

I'm living in Kelantan

Kelantan is a state in Malaysia. The capital is Kota Bharu and the royal seat is Kupang Kerang. The state's honorific name is Darul Naim (Abode of Happiness"). Kelantan is located on the northeastern corner of the peninsula.

KELANTAN FOOD

Nasi Kerabu

Nasi kerabu is a Malaysian rice dish, in which blue rice is served with dried fish or fried chicken

Kuih Akok

Kuih akok is a typical Malaysian sweet food from Kelantan and Terengganu. It's often made with eggs, coconut milk and more

Roti Titab

Roti Titab comes topped with a perfect half-boiled egg, with coconut jam spread on each corner of the bread

Nasi Tumpang

Nasi tumpang is a rice dish from Kelantan, Malaysia. The rice is wrapped in a cone form with banana leaf

Laksam

Laksam Laksei (Laksang) is a type of common dish from Kelantan. It is made from rice flour and eaten with gravy

Mee Celup

Mee Celup is a noodle dish from Kelantan.It comes with lime and assorted sauces on the side to enhance the flavour
